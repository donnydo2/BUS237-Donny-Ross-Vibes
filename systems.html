How to Use the Warehouse Command Center

Step 1: Navigate to the Dashboard

Click on the "Dashboard" tab in the navigation bar. This will take you to the main inventory management page.

Step 2: Select an Item

Use the dropdown menu under "Add Item" to choose the warehouse item you would like to update.

Step 3: Enter Quantity

Type the quantity of the selected item into the quantity input field. Only positive numbers are accepted.

Step 4: Click "Add Item"

Press the "Add Item" button to update the inventory. The system will automatically:

  • Update the inventory list
  • Update the visual bar chart
  • Save the data to your browser

Step 5: View Visualization

The bar chart automatically reflects inventory levels in real time. Each bar represents the quantity of a specific item.

Step 6: Remove Items

To delete an item from the inventory, click the "Remove" button next to the item in the list.

Step 7: Data Persistence

The system uses localStorage to save inventory data. This means your inventory remains saved even if the page refreshes.
