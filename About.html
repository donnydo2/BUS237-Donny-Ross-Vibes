Donny Ross

My name is Donny Ross. I am a second-year SFU student majoring in Economics and minoring in Statistics. Economics has always been a strong interest of mine, and I enjoy exploring how economic systems shape decision-making, markets, and policy.

Some of my passions include reading, hiking, exercising, and continuing to deepen my understanding of economics. Outside of school, I have worked in several industries including trades, the fitness industry, and currently in the liquor industry.

I have also lived in Africa, where I traveled and worked at a local school teaching fitness and health. That experience broadened my perspective and strengthened my interest in global economic development.

My long-term goal is to use my Economics degree to work for the Bank of Canada and contribute to national economic policy and financial stability.
